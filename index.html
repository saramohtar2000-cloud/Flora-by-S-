Our Story

Rooted in Nature,
Made by Hand

Flora by S began with a simple ritual: pressing flowers from walks, gardens, and quiet afternoons, then giving them a second life as something you can wear close to your heart. Every petal has already lived a full life in the sun before it reaches you—which is exactly why no two pieces will ever be the same. Each one is slow-made, never rushed, and inspired by the quiet beauty of the natural world.

  • Handcrafted with real, ethically gathered flowers
  • Every piece is completely one-of-a-kind
  • Slow-made, never mass-produced
  • Inspired by the quiet beauty of the natural world
  • Designed to celebrate self-love and individuality
